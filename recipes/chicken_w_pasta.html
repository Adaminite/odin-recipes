Chicken and Pasta

Description

A simply, yet exquisite, meal to add to your repertoire. It makes a fantastic dinner

Ingredients

  • Two chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Bread crumbs
  • Pasta of choice
  • 12 cups of water

Materials

  • Oven
  • Pot
  • Wooden spoon
  • Tin foil
  • Baking pan
  • Strainer

Steps

  1. Wash chicken breasts and dry them.
  2. Generously cover both sides of the breasts with bread crumbs.
  3. Preheat oven to 400 deg F.
  4. Place tin foil on baking pan and place chicken breasts on foil.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes.
  6. Fill pot with 12 cups of water and bring water to a boil.
  7. Pour in pasta and cook for 10-12 minutes.
  8. Strain water from pasta in strainer.
