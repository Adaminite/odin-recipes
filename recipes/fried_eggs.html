Fried Eggs

Description

A breakfast classic not for the faint of heart. It treads the fine line between solid and slimy, with the crunchy white and runny yolk. However, it is arguably the best way to cook an egg.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper (optional)

Materials

  • Non-stick pan
  • Spatula
  • Bowl

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl
  2. Pour olive oil into pan and heat at medium temperature
  3. When oil is hot, pour eggs into the pan
  4. Once white solidifies, take eggs out of pan and place on a plate
  5. Lightly sprinkle with salt and pepper
