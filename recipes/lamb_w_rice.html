Lamb and Rice

Description

My personal favorite: so simple yet delicious.

Ingredients

  • Ground lamb
  • Olive oil
  • Rice
  • Water
  • Salt and pepper

Materials

  • Pot
  • Pan
  • Spatula

Steps

  1. Put ground lamb in pan and break it up.
  2. Drizzle oil on it, sprinkle salt and pepper on it, and cook on med-high
  3. Close heat after 10 minutes.
  4. Place rice in pot and fill until water level is 1/2 thumb above the top of the rice
  5. Cook until water is fully cooked out.
  6. Serve with lamb on plate and enjoy.
